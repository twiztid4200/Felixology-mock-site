IN MEMORY OF FELIX BROW-WESTBROOK

The Story of Felix

Felix was an enchanted soul, a brilliant young artist, and a passionate lover of literature who left us at just 29 after a courageous battle with mesothelioma.

Felixology was born from love — a whimsical, independent bookstore in Maynard, Massachusetts that honors his memory every single day. Through curated books, meaningful gifts, original art, creative workshops, local history, and heartfelt community gatherings, we keep his magical mind alive.

At the heart of everything we do are Felix’s 8 Foundations: Creativity • Community • Literature • Art & Expression • Local History • The Magical Mind • Healing & Hope • Whimsy & Wonder.
